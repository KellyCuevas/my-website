Hi, I'm a flutist and educator in New York City.

A little bit about me.

I am a flexible musician who enjoys playing a wide range of styles, from Baroque to Modern on flute, piccolo, alto flute, recorder, and baroque flute (A=415). I am available for live and remote recording, private or public functions, as well as orchestral, pit, and wind band leader and section work. I have a large network of outstanding musicians to call upon for all of your event needs.

I am currently principal flute with the Richmond County Orchestra and Riverside Opera Company. I can also be found playing flute with the Park Avenue Chamber Symphony, Litha Symphony, and various chamber and orchestral groups throughout the New York City metropolitan area. I teach over 500 students as a band director in the New York City public schools, and I am available for private lessons in flute, conducting, and music theory.
